Music Box - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Music Box ("we", "our" or "the application") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our music player application.

Information We Collect

Local Data

  • Music Library: The application accesses your local audio files to create your music library. This data remains only on your device.
  • Metadata: Titles, artists, albums, artwork, and other information from your audio files.
  • User Preferences: Your playlists, favorites, listening history, and application settings.
  • Custom Artwork: Images you choose to customize your songs.

Advertising Data

The application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Interactions with advertisements

For more information: Google Privacy Policy

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to improve the application:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data

How We Use Your Data

  • Application Operation: To play your local music, manage your playlists, and personalize your experience.
  • Service Improvement: To fix bugs and improve features.
  • Personalized Advertising: AdMob may use your data to display relevant advertisements.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All your music data, playlists, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Remote Servers: We do not transfer or store your personal data on our servers.
  • Custom Artwork: Stored in the application folder on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements
  • Google Firebase: For anonymous analytics
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (directly in the application)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the application or via settings)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (in Android Settings → Google → Ads)

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Your music data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Children

Music Box does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

Third-Party Services

This application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements. AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Google Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymous usage statistics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: Required for app functionality. Google Privacy Policy

Application Permissions

Music Box requests the following permissions to function properly:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_AUDIO: To access and play your music files stored on your device.
  • INTERNET: To display advertisements and send anonymous analytics.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check internet connectivity for ads.
  • MODIFY_AUDIO_SETTINGS: To access the system equalizer.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To play music in the background when the app is minimized.
  • REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS: To prevent the system from stopping music playback to save battery.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display playback controls in the notification area.

Data Retention

All your data (playlists, favorites, listening history, preferences) is stored locally on your device. This data is retained until you uninstall the application or manually delete it through the app settings. We do not store any of your data on remote servers.

Your Choices

  • Advertising: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Analytics: Firebase Analytics collects anonymous data that cannot be disabled individually, but no personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Permissions: You can revoke permissions at any time through Android Settings → Apps → Music Box → Permissions. Note that revoking storage permission will prevent the app from accessing your music.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed and to whom
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

Note: Music Box does not sell your personal information. All data remains on your device.

European Union Users (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: You can access your data directly within the app.
  • Right to Rectification: You can modify your playlists and preferences in the app.
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Your playlists and preferences are stored locally and you can access them through file managers.
  • Right to Object: You can object to personalized advertising through Android settings.

Legal basis for processing: Legitimate interest (app functionality) and Consent (advertising).

International Data Transfers

Your music and personal data remain on your device and are not transferred internationally. However, anonymous analytics data collected by Firebase may be processed by Google in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of Music Box after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Do Not Track

Music Box does not track users across third-party websites. However, our advertising partners (Google AdMob) may track users for advertising purposes. You can enable "Limit Ad Tracking" in your device settings to reduce tracking.
