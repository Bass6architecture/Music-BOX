Introduction

Music Box ("we", "our" or "the application") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our music player application.

Information We Collect

Local Data

  • Music Library: The application accesses your local audio files to create your music library. This data remains only on your device.
  • Metadata: Titles, artists, albums, artwork, and other information from your audio files.
  • User Preferences: Your playlists, favorites, listening history, and application settings.
  • Custom Artwork: Images you choose to customize your songs.

Advertising Data

The application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Interactions with advertisements

For more information: Google Privacy Policy

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to improve the application:

  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash reports
  • Performance data

How We Use Your Data

  • Application Operation: To play your local music, manage your playlists, and personalize your experience.
  • Service Improvement: To fix bugs and improve features.
  • Personalized Advertising: AdMob may use your data to display relevant advertisements.

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: All your music data, playlists, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Remote Servers: We do not transfer or store your personal data on our servers.
  • Custom Artwork: Stored in the application folder on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements
  • Google Firebase: For anonymous analytics
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (directly in the application)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the application or via settings)
  • Opt out of personalized ads (in Android Settings → Google → Ads)

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. Your music data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Children

Music Box does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
